## Why I Chose to Build GridScoop

Every day, thousands of new products appear across online marketplaces.

Finding them isn't difficult.

Understanding **which products actually matter** is.

While working with global consumer brands, I repeatedly saw teams spending hours manually tracking launches, comparing pricing, identifying competitors, and preparing reports before they could make a business decision.

The bottleneck wasn't the data.

It was turning scattered information into actionable intelligence.

GridScoop is my attempt to solve that problem.

Instead of building another dashboard, I'm building a platform that helps teams discover products, monitor competitors, and surface insights automatically using AI-assisted workflows.

I chose to build this because I've seen the problem first-hand across enterprise clients, and I believe this workflow should be automated—not repeated manually every week.

---

# The Problem

Product, category, and strategy teams often struggle to answer questions like:

- Which competitors launched new products this week?
- What pricing changes happened yesterday?
- Which brands are gaining visibility?
- What emerging trends deserve attention?
- Which insights are signal, and which are simply noise?

Today these questions usually require:

- Manual searches
- Spreadsheet comparisons
- Multiple dashboards
- Hours of analyst effort

My goal is to reduce that process to minutes.

---

# Vision

GridScoop aims to become an AI-powered product intelligence platform that combines:

- Product discovery
- Competitive intelligence
- Pricing analysis
- AI-generated insights
- Automated reporting

Rather than simply showing data, the objective is to help users make faster product decisions.
